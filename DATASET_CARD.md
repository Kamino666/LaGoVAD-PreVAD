## PreVAD
Code repo: https://github.com/Kamino666/LaGoVAD-PreVAD

PreVAD (Pre-training Video Anomaly Dataset) is a large-scale and diverse video anomaly detection dataset, featuring 35,279 annotated videos with multi-level category labels and descriptions that explicitly define anomalies.
